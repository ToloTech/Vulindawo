About Us

Vulindawo (fathers first name) means: Open up places . We took his name literally and expanded on
his legacy, incorporating the families strengths in creating a family business.

Our father started his business in the taxi industry in 1980 and he’s been going strong until present.
Times have changed and are still changing and to keep up you can now get most services right here on our website.

At Vulindawo we are creating a place where no request is too small, big or crazy because we believe in ensuring your needs and wants are met to your expectations!

We guarantee you personalised and professional services – no request is the same, we will treat your request with the utmost professionalism on a budget-friendly fee.
